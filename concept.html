!MIRROR


Welcome to my p5.js video exploration project! In this project, I have created five different video filters using the p5.js library. Each filter has a unique style and inspiration.


The first filter is TEXTIFY!, which allows the user to input their own text and see it displayed in a creative way. The second filter is WARHOL!, inspired by the famous artist Andy Warhol and his bold, colorful pop art style. The third filter is POP!, another pop art-inspired filter with bright colors and patterns. The fourth filter is VERTEX!, which utilizes real colors from the video to create a unique and dynamic effect. The fifth filter is SKETCH!, a black and white vertex filter that creates a sketch-like effect on the video.


I had a lot of fun exploring the capabilities of p5.js and experimenting with different filter styles. I hope you enjoy trying out the filters and discovering the possibilities of video exploration with p5.js.
